## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Always draw boundary first** before placing panels
2. **Use grid for bulk**, manual for precision
3. **Drag panels** to avoid obstacles (vents, chimneys)
4. **Save frequently** - designs stored locally
5. **Test GPS outdoors** for best accuracy

---

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Can I place panels without GPS tracking?**  
A: Yes! GPS is optional. You can manually navigate the map.

**Q: How many panels can I place?**  
A: Unlimited! Grid mode can place 200+ panels in seconds.

**Q: Do panels sync between 2D and 3D?**  
A: Yes! Coordinates are converted automatically.

**Q: Can I save my design?**  
A: Yes! Click "Save Design" in toolbar → exports JSON.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**  
A: Yes! Touch controls and GPS work great on phones.
