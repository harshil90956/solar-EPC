## 🎯 Quick Actions

### Jump to Your City
1. Click any preset button:
   - **Delhi NCR** → 28.54°N, 77.33°E
   - **Mumbai** → 19.07°N, 72.87°E  
   - **Bangalore** → 12.97°N, 77.59°E
   - And 5 more cities!

### Navigate to Specific Building
1. **Get coordinates:**
   - Open Google Maps website
   - Right-click on your building
   - Click the coordinates shown
2. **Enter in control panel:**
   - Paste Lat (e.g., `28.54317`)
   - Paste Lng (e.g., `77.335763`)
3. **Click "Go to Location"**
4. **Map centers on your building!**

### Add Solar Roof
1. **Center crosshair on building roof**
2. **Click "Add Roof at This Location"**
3. **Switch to [3D] view** to see it!
4. **Customize and add panels** in 3D

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Use Satellite View
- Toggle **[Satellite]** button for roof visibility
- Best for identifying buildings

### Tip 2: Zoom to 20
- Use zoom slider (10-21)
- Level 20 = perfect roof detail
- Level 21 = maximum zoom

### Tip 3: Get Coordinates Fast
- Google Maps → Right-click building → Click coordinates
- Copy → Paste into control panel
- One-click jump!
