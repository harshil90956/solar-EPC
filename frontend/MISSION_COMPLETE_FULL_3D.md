Open: `http://localhost:3002`

### **2. Navigate to Survey Page**
- Click **Survey** in the main navigation
- You'll see the enhanced 3D environment immediately

### **3. Explore View Modes**
- **3D Map** - Full 3D environment with drawing tools
- **3D Scene** - Enhanced architectural visualization  
- **Split View** - Dual 3D panels
- **Overview** - Bird's eye 3D perspective

### **4. Use 3D Drawing Tools**
1. Select **Draw Area** from the toolbar
2. Click points in the 3D space to create building outline
3. Visual guides show your progress
4. Double-click or click near first point to complete

### **5. Navigate in 3D Space**
- **Zoom:** Mouse wheel (unlimited range)
- **Rotate:** Click and drag (full 360°)
- **Pan:** Right-click and drag
